The Pattern in Practice

Four problems.
One consistent approach.

Case 01

Turning a Cost Center into a Margin Engine

Inherited a global services organization running on a traditional break-fix model — low margin, reactive, and structurally unprofitable in several markets. Rebuilt the entire operating model: rationalized SKUs, renegotiated supplier contracts, amended SLAs, and converted the remaining business into a proactive, software-supported revenue stream. Services revenue contribution grew from 5% to 9%.

$24M operating margin expansion · $25M structural efficiency gains · 13% YoY growth
Case 02

Making M&A Actually Deliver Unified Platforms

Most acquisitions create product silos — separate teams, separate roadmaps, no shared architecture. Diagnosed this as the root cause of unrealized synergies across multiple transactions. Built a unified gateway strategy from scratch: designed and shipped enterprise integration products that connected devices, clinical workflows, and engineering systems into a single operating layer at global scale.

$15B+ in M&A value shaped across multiple transactions
Case 04

Building NYC's Public Health Infrastructure — Through Hurricane Sandy

Led two concurrent NYC mandates: a HEAL NY state grant program deploying full-stack health IT across 22 locations and 200+ community organizations, and a Bloomberg-funded migration from Siemens Unity to Epic across 11 hospitals. When Hurricane Sandy buried the primary data center under 8 feet of water, the team executed failover to Sungard NJ and kept all hospitals running.

22 sites · 200+ CBOs · SHIN-NY integrated · Sandy failover executed
Case 05

Integrating an Acquired Health System While the Hospitals Kept Running

Hired to integrate Continuum Health Partners into Mount Sinai — 8 hospitals, $60M+ IT budget, disparate EMR, billing, and lab systems. Built the unified Epic framework, connected the system to Healthix RHIO, and delivered Meaningful Use Stage 2 attestation protecting millions in Medicare incentive payments — all with live hospitals on both sides of the integration.

MU Stage 2 achieved · Healthix RHIO · Full Epic unification · Promoted to Division CIO
